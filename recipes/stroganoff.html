Chicken Stroganoff

Description

This chicken stroganoff is so good and so easy to make in the slow cooker on a busy day.
It is a simple, creamy sauce with cubed chicken breast. Serve over hot cooked rice or pasta.

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time 5 hrs 30 mins
  • Total Time: 5 hrs 40 mins
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves-cubed
  • â…› cup butter
  • 1 (.7 ounce) package dry italian-style salad dressing mix+
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Put chicken, butter, and dressing mix in a slow cooker.
  3. Mix together well and cook on low for 6 to 6 hours.
  4. Add condensed soup and cream chesse. Mix together and cook on
    High until heated through and warm, about 30 minutes.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
